About StayClean

StayClean is a local NGO in Muyenga community that provides waste management, disposal and sanitation services to the community. These services include:-

  • Emptying latrines and septic tanks
  • Rubbish collection
You can Subscribe to our services by making an application and we'll get back to you with a response.
