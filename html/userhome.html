Why StayClean?


StayClean is urbanizing more rapidly than any other part of the planet, its 1.1 billion citizens will most likely double in number by 2050 and more than 80% of that increase will occur in cities especially slums. One result of a rapid urbanization is the problem of waste.

An average person generates 1kg of solid waste per day, the volume is huge compared to the rate (40-50%) at which it’s collected. With such rapid urbanization in many African cities, it’ll be seamlessly impossible for waste collectors in Africa to service the growing population effectively without better technology tools.
